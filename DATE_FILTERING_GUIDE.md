## ðŸŽ¨ How to Use

### **Location:**
The date filter appears at the top of the dashboard, just below the unit selection (NICU/PICU) and above the statistics cards.

### **Step-by-Step:**

#### **For Preset Periods (Today, Week, Month):**
1. Click the **"Filter by Admission Date"** dropdown
2. Select your desired period (Today, This Week, This Month)
3. Dashboard automatically updates with filtered data
4. Statistics and charts reflect the selected period

#### **For Specific Months:**
1. Click the **"Filter by Admission Date"** dropdown
2. Scroll through the list of months (last 12 months shown)
3. Select the month you want (e.g., "October 2024")
4. Dashboard shows only patients admitted in that month

#### **For Custom Date Range:**
1. Click the **"Filter by Admission Date"** dropdown
2. Select **"Custom"** from the bottom of the list
3. Two date picker fields appear:
   - **Start Date**: Click and select the beginning date
   - **End Date**: Click and select the ending date
4. Dashboard filters to show patients admitted between these dates
5. End date is automatically limited to be after start date
